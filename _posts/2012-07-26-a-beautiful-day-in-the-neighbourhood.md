I try to run out by Chautauqua Park about once a week. I consider it an &#8220;easy run&#8221; in that I don&#8217;t pay attention to pace or try to push myself, but it&#8217;s not really that easy! Pretty well everytime I run, I think how I should have brought a camera to capture the experience, and today I finally did!
